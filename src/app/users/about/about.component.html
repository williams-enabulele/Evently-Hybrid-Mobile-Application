This study focuses on addressing the challenges facing the dissemination of information in the University among lecturers, students and non-academic staff as it makes room for the introduction of an event broadcast mobile application that takes into account the various preferences of numerous categories of users.

At the moment, in most Universities, information regarding upcoming events is disseminated via notice boards, letters submitted in hardcopy, word of mouth, and more recently, the use of WhatsApp groups for those who are already in the group.
